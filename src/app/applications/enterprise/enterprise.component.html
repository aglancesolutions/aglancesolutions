WORK MATTERS

Work-related pain and discomfort is reducing the productivity of our workforce and draining expert work-related knowledge from operational teams at an alarming rate. Our software helps identify common drivers and creates an intelligent starting point for implementing new strategies to combat work-related pain.

RAPID OVERVIEW

Gain insight by mapping and tracking work-related pain. Pain and discomfort maps are integrated into precision-reports to reveal common pain and discomfort patterns in relation to equipment, work-tasks and stations. Employees can submit a pain report at any time from anywhere via phone, computer or tablet.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

The need for work-related pain, discomfort and injury assessment has never been stronger. Pain is a warning signifying something may be wrong. However, non-painful and unusual sensations or discomforts, such as numbing or tingling, are also warnings. Our software enables health and safety teams to track and identify the silent, slower accumulating pain and discomforts associated with work-related (chronic) pain.
