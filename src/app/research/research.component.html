SCIENTIFIC PUBLICATIONS AND RESEARCH

The Navigate Pain software originated in the laboratory as a tool to better understand pain and discomfort. Today, Navigate Pain is being used in a range of scientific research projects.

We support research addressing where-related questions about pain and discomfort.
